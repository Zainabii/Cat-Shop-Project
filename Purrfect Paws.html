Available Cats:

Black Shorthair - $200

The Black Shorthair is a sleek and elegant breed known for its short, dense coat that is predominantly black. They have a friendly and affectionate nature, making them great companions. Black Shorthairs are generally low-maintenance cats and are known for their adaptability to various living situations.

Siamese - $300

The Siamese breed is recognized for its striking blue almond-shaped eyes and distinctive color points on its ears, face, paws, and tail. They are known for their intelligence, curiosity, and social nature. Siamese cats are often vocal and enjoy interacting with their owners. They require regular mental and physical stimulation.

Persian - $400

Persian cats are characterized by their long, luxurious coats, round faces, and gentle demeanor. They have a calm and placid temperament, making them ideal indoor companions. Persian cats require regular grooming to maintain their coat's health and appearance.

Maine Coon - $500

Maine Coons are one of the largest domestic cat breeds, known for their impressive size and tufted ears. They have a friendly and outgoing personality and are often referred to as "gentle giants." Maine Coons have thick, water-repellent fur, making them well-suited to colder climates.

Ragdoll - $350

Ragdoll cats are known for their docile and relaxed nature, often going limp when picked up, hence the name "Ragdoll." They have stunning blue eyes and soft, semi-longhair coats that come in various patterns and colors. Ragdolls are typically affectionate and enjoy being around people.

Bengal - $450

The Bengal breed is known for its striking appearance, resembling a miniature leopard. They have a distinctive spotted or marbled coat that comes in various colors, including brown, silver, and snow. Bengals are energetic, intelligent, and require plenty of mental and physical stimulation.
